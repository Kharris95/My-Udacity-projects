Pixels is a unique and engaging game built on the Ronin blockchain, where players create, trade, and showcase pixel art in a decentralized environment. Each pixel art piece is represented as a unique NFT (non-fungible token) on the Ronin blockchain, ensuring ownership and scarcity. The game combines creativity with strategy, allowing players to build and curate their pixel art collection while participating in a vibrant, player-driven economy. With Ronin's efficient and low-cost transactions, Pixels offers an accessible and fun way to explore digital art and blockchain technology.

In Pixels on the Ronin blockchain, players can earn $PIXEL tokens through active participation and creative contributions. By engaging in various in-game activities such as creating and trading pixel art NFTs, completing challenges, and participating in community events, players can accumulate $PIXEL tokens. These tokens not only serve as a reward but also enable players to trade, invest, or enhance their gameplay experience within the ecosystem. Earning $PIXEL allows players to directly benefit from their creativity and engagement in the vibrant Pixels community.
